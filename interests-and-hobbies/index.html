LEGO Sets and MOCs

In April 2020, I started to get involved more with LEGO. I think a huge part of this is because we’re all stuck inside from the COVID-19 epidemic. But, I discovered a tool called LEGO Digital Designer (LDD), and you can use it to build your own LEGO sets. So, I thought I’d share some of the LEGO sets I’ve built and designed (also called MOCs—or My Own Creations), as well as some LEGO sets I’ve purchased and played around with. See my dedicated LEGO page for more!

For a list of the shows I worked with and the training I participated in, see my dedicated Theatre page. Huge shoutout and thank you to my family for always being supportive of my theatre pasttime and for always being willing to help me out.
